OPEN-KNOW-WHERE (OKW) CHAPTER

Vision

Open-Know-Where (OKW) aspires to be the global platform that empowers individuals and organizations in the maker and manufacturing ecosystem by providing accessible, comprehensive, and contextually relevant data.

Mission

Our mission is to democratize knowledge, foster collaboration, and catalyze innovation in the maker community by curating and sharing data about locations, resources, and projects.

Principles

1. Openness

OKW is committed to being an open platform. Data contributed to OKW is encouraged to be open and accessible to the community, promoting transparency and shared knowledge.

2. Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and strive to be inclusive. OKW is a platform for all makers, regardless of their background, location, or expertise. We encourage the participation of individuals and organizations worldwide.

3. Privacy and Security

OKW respects the privacy and security of its users. We are committed to implementing robust measures to protect the personal and sensitive information shared on the platform.

4. Collaboration

Collaboration is at the core of OKW. We encourage partnerships, discussions, and initiatives that contribute to the growth and sustainability of the maker ecosystem.

5. Community-Driven

OKW is a community-driven platform. Input from the community is highly valued, and decisions regarding the platform's development and features are made collectively.

Guidelines

1. Data Integrity

Contributors are encouraged to provide accurate and up-to-date information. OKW relies on the integrity of its data to be a valuable resource for the community.

2. Respect

Users are expected to treat each other with respect and courtesy. OKW is a space for constructive conversations and shared learning.

3. Attribution

Users contributing data to OKW retain ownership of their contributions. Proper attribution is given to contributors, and their intellectual property rights are respected.

4. Continuous Improvement

OKW is committed to continuous improvement. We actively seek feedback from the community to enhance the platform's functionality and user experience.

Governance

The governance of OKW involves an open and transparent decision-making process. Major decisions, changes to guidelines, and the appointment of key roles are discussed and decided upon collectively by the community.

Conclusion

Open-Know-Where is a community-driven initiative with a vision to strengthen the global maker community. By adhering to these principles and guidelines, we aim to create a collaborative and empowering platform for makers worldwide.
